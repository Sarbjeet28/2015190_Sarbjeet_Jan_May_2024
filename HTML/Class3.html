Introduction of Artificial Intelligence

AI stands for Artificial Intelligence. It refers to the development of computer systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, speech recognition, and language translation, among others. AI aims to create machines that can mimic human cognitive functions and adapt to new information or stimuli.

There are two main types of AI:

Narrow AI (Weak AI):

This type of AI is designed and trained for a particular task. It can perform specific functions, but it lacks the broad cognitive abilities of humans. Examples include voice assistants like Siri or Alexa and image recognition systems.

General AI (Strong AI):

This is a more advanced form of AI that can understand, learn, and apply knowledge across a wide range of tasks, similar to human intelligence. General AI is still largely theoretical and hasn't been achieved yet.
